Fans in Tears App

Fans in Tears is a space for football fun and engagement.

Features:

  • Memes. Daily funny football memes and videos.
  • Football players social feed. Learn more about a daily life of the most popular professional footballers.
  • Chats. Select one of the tournaments and enter a telegram group with all the fans engaged in the selected league.
  • Telegram Media Bot. All chat groups have FansInTearsMediaBot inside which can get latest media posts by hashtag from Instagram or Twitter. Want to get a photo from the Portugal-France match? Just ask the bot for the latest media by the porfra hashtag.

We want this app to be a greate place for football fans engagement so we keep adding new features.
